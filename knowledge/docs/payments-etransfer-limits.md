# Interac e-Transfer limits and processing times

## Limits

Default e-Transfer limits are $3,000 per transaction, $10,000 per day, and $20,000 per 7 days. Premium chequing customers have $5,000 / $15,000 / $30,000. Limits reset at midnight Eastern Time, not on a rolling window.

## Processing times

Transfers to recipients registered for Autodeposit complete within 5 minutes. Transfers requiring a security answer can take up to 30 minutes to send and expire after 30 days unclaimed. Expired transfers are refunded automatically within 1 business day.

## Cancelling

An e-Transfer can be cancelled free of charge any time before the recipient deposits it, from Activity > Pending transfers. After deposit, a transfer cannot be reversed; disputes go through the fraud team.

# Card limits and PIN management

## Spending limits

Debit purchase limits default to $2,000 per day at point of sale and $1,000 per day at ATMs. Customers can lower limits themselves in Cards > Limits; increases above the default require a branch visit or verified phone call.

## PIN changes

PINs can be changed at any NorthStar ATM or in the app under Cards > Change PIN. Three incorrect PIN attempts at a terminal block chip transactions until the card is used with the correct PIN at a NorthStar ATM. Blocked PINs cannot be reset over the phone.

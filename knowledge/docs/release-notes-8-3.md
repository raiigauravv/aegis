# Release notes — NorthStar app 8.3

## New in 8.3

Version 8.3 (released 2026-05-14) adds virtual card numbers for online purchases, a redesigned approval flow for e-Transfers over $1,000, and Tap to Pay on Android. Virtual cards are issued instantly under Cards > Virtual cards and can be frozen independently of the physical card.

## Fixed issues

8.3 fixes the payees-screen crash on Android 15 (introduced in 8.2.0), corrects rounding on FX previews, and restores missing push notifications on Pixel devices after OS updates.

## Known limitations

Virtual cards cannot yet be added to Apple Pay (planned for 8.4). Tap to Pay requires Android 13 or newer.

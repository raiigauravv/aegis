# Statements and tax documents

## Statements

Monthly e-statements are available in the app under Documents on the 3rd business day of each month and are retained for 7 years. Paper statements cost $2.50/month. Statement PDFs are official documents accepted for proof of address.

## Tax slips

T5 slips for interest income over $50 are issued by the last day of February and appear under Documents > Tax. RRSP contribution receipts are issued twice: March (for first-60-days contributions) and January (for the prior calendar year).
